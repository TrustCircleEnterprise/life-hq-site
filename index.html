📈 Trading Roadmap

7 consecutive profitable days → consistent weekly withdrawals. GBP/JPY & XAU/USD.

Your Forex Dashboard has live signals, auto S/R, and your trade journal. Open it before every session. 4/4 confluence = trade. Anything less = wait.
Daily Checklist — Trading
Pre-Session (6–8 AM)
Morning Setup
  • 1Check economic calendar — news events today
  • 2Open Forex Dashboard — check S/R levels
  • 3Note current GBP/JPY and XAU/USD bias
  • 4Set your max loss for the day (rule: stop at 2 losses)
  • 5Confirm you are in the right mindset to trade
During Session
Trade Execution Rules
  • 1Wait for 4/4 confluence — never force a trade
  • 2Confirm on TradingView before entering
  • 3Risk only 4% per trade ($16 on $400 account)
  • 4Set SL beyond S/R + 5 pip buffer immediately
  • 5Max 2 trades/day. Stop after 2 losses — no exceptions
Post-Session
Review & Journal
  • 1Log every trade in your journal (dashboard auto-syncs)
  • 2Note: did you follow all 4 rules?
  • 3Screenshot any setups (winning or losing)
  • 4Rate your discipline 1–10
  • 5Identify 1 thing to improve tomorrow
7-Day Goal → Withdrawals
The Path to Weekly Income
  • 1Track profitable days in a row — reset resets count
  • 2Focus on CONSISTENCY not big wins — 1.5R every day
  • 3Hit 7 profitable days → request first withdrawal
  • 4Reinvest portion to grow account size
  • 5Build to weekly withdrawal rhythm

💳 Credit & Finance Roadmap

620+ score → pull home equity → Tesla + home improvements + clear $19k.

Daily Checklist — Credit
Step 1 — Immediately
Know Your Exact Position
  • 1Pull free report: AnnualCreditReport.com (all 3 bureaus)
  • 2List every negative item with date and amount
  • 3Calculate your current utilization ratio (balance ÷ limit)
  • 4Note your current score on each bureau
  • 5Target: get all 3 bureaus to 620+
Step 2 — Fastest Wins
Boost Score Quickly
  • 1Pay down credit cards to under 30% utilization (fastest boost)
  • 2Under 10% utilization = maximum scoring benefit
  • 3Dispute any errors or inaccurate items in writing
  • 4Never miss a payment — set all minimums to autopay
  • 5Ask for goodwill removal of any late payments
Step 3 — Hit 620
Home Equity Loan Approval
  • 1Get home appraised (your free & clear home is your asset)
  • 2Calculate 80% LTV — that's your max pull amount
  • 3Shop at least 3 lenders: banks, credit unions, online lenders
  • 4Compare rates — fixed HELOC vs lump-sum home equity loan
  • 5Apply and close — funds arrive within 30–45 days
Step 4 — The Payout Plan
Use Equity Wisely
  • 1Clear $19k credit card debt first (eliminate high-interest)
  • 2Fund key home improvements (adds value to the property)
  • 3Purchase Tesla (factor monthly payment into budget)
  • 4Keep emergency fund untouched — 3 months expenses
  • 5Make every HELOC payment on time to protect the home
Credit Score Milestones
Get to 580 — subprime threshold
Focus: utilization under 30%, no new missed payments
Hit 620 — equity loan eligible
Most home equity lenders accept 620+
Reach 650 — better rates unlocked
Significantly lower interest rates on any loan
700+ — prime borrower
Best rates on everything, SBA loan eligibility
